The reason we named this roof sahara is because...

In the scorching heat of July, just north of Birmingham, there was a roof—800 square meters of challenge dubbed "Sahara" for good reason. With temperatures soaring past 30 degrees Celsius, it was a blistering task. But four determined souls, including two gutsy women, weren't backing down.

Armed with tools and grit, they took on the project head-on. Every day felt like a showdown with the sun, but they kept at it. Weeks flew by, and slowly Sahara transformed.

With each nail hammered and tile laid, their determination only grew. And when they finally finished, it wasn't just a roof anymore—it was a testament to their hard work.

Now, whenever we look at Sahara, we're reminded of their incredible feat. It's not just a roof; it's a symbol of perseverance and teamwork. We salute them for their dedication, proving that even the toughest challenges can be conquered with enough determination.
